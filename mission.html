The Mission of Water Footprint Calculator

Our mission is to raise awareness about the growing concern of water consumption in the production of consumer goods. We aim to educate users on the hidden water usage behind their everyday choices and encourage more sustainable habits.

Why It Matters

Water scarcity is a global challenge. Understanding water consumption is key to a sustainable future. Hereâ€™s why:

  • Over 2.1 billion people worldwide lack access to clean drinking water.
  • Water scarcity affects 4 out of 10 people globally.
  • The water footprint concept helps us quantify the unseen consumption of water in production processes.

Our Goals

We strive to achieve the following:

  • Educate individuals about their personal water footprint.
  • Encourage the adoption of water-saving habits.
  • Promote sustainable water usage in production and consumption.

Our Actions

To fulfill our mission, we have developed the Water Footprint Calculator, which helps users understand how much water is used to produce different goods.
